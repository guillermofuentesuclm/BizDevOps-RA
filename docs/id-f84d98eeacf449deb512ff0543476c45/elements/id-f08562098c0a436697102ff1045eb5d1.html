Product Owner (Alignment Manager) ()
Definition: In BizDevOps, this role is pivotal in ensuring that the product aligns with business needs and creates added value. The primary focus of the Product Owner shifts towards facilitating IT-business alignment, emphasizing their responsibility to align IT strategies closely with business objectives. This redefined focus expands their traditional role of bridging the gap between business stakeholders and the IT team, ensuring that the product delivers maximum value in line with business priorities.
