MVP Business Process ()
Definition: An MVP (Minimum Viable Product) business process is the simplest, most streamlined version of a business process that achieves its primary objectives. It includes only the essential steps and elements needed to validate the process's effectiveness, gather feedback, and make iterative improvements with minimal resources and risk.
