IT/Business Alignment ()
Definition: An IT/Business Alignment Driver is a motivating factor that ensures IT strategies and services are integrated with and support business goals. This alignment enhances efficiency, drives competitive advantage, and creates value for the organization by ensuring that IT capabilities directly contribute to business success.
