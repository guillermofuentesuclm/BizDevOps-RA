IT and business are aligned ()
Definition: IT and Business are Aligned is an assessment indicating that IT initiatives, resources, and strategies effectively support and enhance business goals. This alignment ensures cohesive operations, maximizes efficiency, and drives organizational success by ensuring that IT contributions are directly aligned with business needs and objectives.
